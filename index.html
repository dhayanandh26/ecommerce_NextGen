Our Story

NextGen Store was founded in 2024 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to premium, authentic products without compromise. We noticed that Indian consumers were being offered either counterfeit goods or sky-high prices — and we decided to change that.

We hand-pick every product in our catalog, test it, verify its authenticity, and negotiate the best prices directly with brands and authorized distributors. Every item you see on NextGen has been carefully evaluated by our team for quality, value, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across India and are growing every month. We're proud to have maintained a 4.6-star average rating across all our products, with thousands of verified customer reviews.

Our Mission

To democratize access to premium products with transparent pricing, authentic goods, and world-class customer experience.

100% Authentic

Every product is sourced directly from authorized dealers and brand partners. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeits.

Fast Delivery

Free delivery on orders above ₹499. Most orders are delivered within 2–4 business days with real-time tracking.

Hassle-Free Returns

30-day no-questions-asked return policy. If you're not satisfied, we'll pick it up and give you a full refund.

24/7 Support

Our customer support team is available around the clock. Reach us via chat, email, or phone — we're always here.

Secure Payments

256-bit SSL encryption on all transactions. We support UPI, cards, net banking, and cash on delivery.

Best Prices

We negotiate directly with brands to offer the lowest prices. Price-match guarantee on all products.
